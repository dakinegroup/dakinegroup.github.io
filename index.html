da Kine Technologies (in short referred here as DKT), is an initiative to fill the gap where Indian SMEs need partners to harness the power of IT in enhancing their productivity, quality and scaling up the business. It is founded by Vineet Kumar Maheshwari, B.E. Electronics (Honours), 1994 passout, from PEC University Of Technology, M.B.A from MDI (Gold Medalist), 2013 passout, PMP certified, Six Sigma Black Belt, Telecom Software Professional.

DKT believes in keeping things simple, move incrementally and with firm foot on the ground. This as a guiding philosophy, it records the requirements, analyse; and finds as well as develop suitable technology to solve the problems. Team has the utmost concern for the return on your investment.

DKT expertise spans across various domains, with expertise depth relevant to the automation in industry applications. Technology domains are - mobile, server side, embedded/data sensing. DKT has done several projects in the areas of e-Governance, ERP, data sensing embedded solutions, mobile apps with backend for online services, community solutions. Team has excellent knowledge on technology, processes, which helps DKT to engage right at the concept level and help take project forward throgh crafting process.

DKT partners in developing solutions, can take up opportunities, where solution and idea need to be converted into proof of concept. DKT understands the sustainability of solutions and believes in documenting that enables initiatives to be reused and reapplied in organization's context.
